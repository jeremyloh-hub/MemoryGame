Memory Game

Choose your Abilities:

Game Rules: Match 2 cards before the timer runs out !

Ability(RemoveOnePair): Allows the player to get 1 matching pair from the start.

Ability(RevealAll): Reveal all the cards for 1 second.

Ability(AddLifePoints): Add additional 3 Life points.

Ability(AddTime): Add additional 30 seconds to the timer.
